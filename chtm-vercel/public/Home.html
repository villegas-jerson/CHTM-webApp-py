About Our Program

Industry Excellence at Heart

The University of Cebu’s College of Hospitality and Tourism Management (CHTM) is a center of excellence dedicated to developing highly competent and globally competitive professionals in the hospitality and tourism industry. The college offers industry-aligned programs that combine academic rigor with practical, hands-on training to ensure graduates are well-prepared for real-world challenges. CHTM emphasizes experiential learning through laboratory classes, industry immersion, internships, and strong partnerships with hotels, resorts, restaurants, travel agencies, and tourism-related organizations. These experiences allow students to apply theoretical knowledge in real workplace settings, enhancing their technical skills, professionalism, and service orientation.

Enrollment Procedures

STEP 1 – Ensure you have an ACTIVE Contact Number and personal Gmail Account. Use your own name for the account holder (e.g., KimTaping@gmail.com). If the chosen email is already owned, add a numeric code (e.g., KimTaping123@gmail.com). This information is needed for the enrollment process.

STEP 2 – FILLING OUT ENROLLMENT FORM AND SUBMISSION OF CREDENTIALS:

  • CLICK the NEW STUDENT button.
  • Fill out the necessary personal data and information.
  • Upload a scanned copy or CLEAR photo image of the following documents (or at least one of the following):

For New Students (Senior High School Graduate):

  1. Form 138
  2. Certificate of Good Moral Character
  3. Photocopy of NSO/PSA Live Birth
  4. Picture with I.D. from previous school (if available)
  5. Certificate of Graduation/ Diploma

For Transferee/Shiftee Students:

  1. Certificate of Transfer Credentials
  2. TOR/ Grade Evaluation
  3. Certificate of Good Moral Character
  4. Photocopy of NSO/PSA Live Birth
  • SELECT the PROGRAM/COURSE/SUBJECTS and SCHEDULES to be enrolled in (including the section).
  • CLICK ACCEPT to the UNDERTAKING.
  • CLICK SUBMIT – personal details and attachments will be forwarded to the Dean/Registrar for evaluation. Upon completion, an I.D. and PASSWORD will be given for re-login and status verification.

STEP 3 – DEAN'S EVALUATION: The Dean will send through the enrollment portal a notice to pay the ENTRANCE FEE of ₱500.00.
