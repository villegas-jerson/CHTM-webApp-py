The Organization

College of Hospitality and Tourism Management (CHTM)

Vision

Democratize Quality Education. Be The Visionary And Industry Leader. Give Hope And Transform Lives. The College of Hospitality and Tourism Management (CHTM) envisions itself as a leading academic institution in the Philippines, committed to excellence in hospitality and tourism education and the development of globally competitive and values-driven professionals.

Mission

University Of Cebu Offers Affordable And Quality Education Responsive To The Demands Of Local And International Communities. University Of Cebu Commits To Serve As Active Catalyst In Providing Efficient And Effective Delivery Of Educational Services, Pursue Excellence In Instruction, Research And Community Service Towards Social And Economic Development As Well As Environmental Sustainability, Acquire, Disseminate And Utilize Appropriate Technology To Enhance The University’s Educational Services, And Foster An Organizational Culture That Nurtures Employee Productivity And Satisfaction.

Objectives

  • To offer programs that are relevant, holistic, and compliant with institutional, regulatory, industry, and accreditation standards that will develop life-long learners
  • To develop social awareness, responsibility, and accountability among stakeholders anchored on instruction, research, and production.
  • To complement the academic programs with holistic and integrated student personnel services
  • To develop a pool of qualified, professional, and motivated faculty in the areas of instruction, research, and community extension.
  • To ensure effective administration and supervision of instructional and learning resources in support of the academic programs.
  • To provide an environment that is safe, functional, and conducive to teaching, learning, and working
  • To ensure operations that are collaborative, sustainable, efficient, and effective in meeting the needs of the institution and its stakeholder

Faculty

Laboratory

Bar and Beverage

The Bar and Beverage program of the College of Hospitality and Tourism Management aims to develop skilled, responsible, and globally competitive professionals in bar and beverage service. It provides students with a strong foundation in beverage knowledge, mixology, and bar operations through a balance of theoretical instruction and hands-on training.

The program emphasizes professionalism, creativity, customer service excellence, and responsible alcohol service while exposing students to current industry trends and standards.

Food Services and Food Preparation

The Food Services and Food Preparation program of the College of Hospitality and Tourism Management is designed to equip students with essential knowledge and practical skills in culinary arts, kitchen operations, and food service management. It emphasizes proper food handling, sanitation, menu planning, and efficient food production while fostering creativity, discipline, and teamwork in a professional kitchen environment.

Through a combination of classroom instruction, laboratory activities, and experiential learning, the program prepares students to deliver quality food products and services that meet industry standards and customer expectations in hotels, restaurants, catering services, and other food service establishments.

more →

Hotel Operations

The Hotel Operations program of the College of Hospitality and Tourism Management focuses on developing students’ competencies in the day-to-day management and operation of hotel establishments. It provides comprehensive training in front office operations, housekeeping, guest relations, and basic hotel management principles.

The program emphasizes service excellence, professionalism, teamwork, and problem-solving skills through a blend of theoretical instruction and hands-on learning. By exposing students to real-world hotel practices and industry standards, the program prepares graduates for effective performance and career growth in hotels, resorts, and related hospitality organizations.
